Welcome To yt.rchat.fun

This is a simple website that allows you to download videos from YouTube.

Instructions

To download a video, simply replace the www.youtube.com in a YouTube URL with yt.rchat.fun.

For example, to download the video
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ,
change the URL to
https://yt.rchat.fun/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ.
